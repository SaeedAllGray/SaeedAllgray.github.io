Navid All Gharaee

Student at Ferdowsi University of Mashhad, Software Engineer & Developer, UI & UX

I am Navid All Gharaee, 21 and I am currently studying computer engineering at Ferdowsi University of Mashhad. Since the very beginning, I was really interested in everything that involves interacting with buttons and screens. When I was 9 years old, my family bought an iMac and I was thrilled by the user interface and the design of this product. My interest was amplified and since then, I started learning new stuff about computers. When I finished the high school, I took the entrance exam and entered the University in the field I love. Beside my academic works and contributions, I worked with the companies and since then, I have gained a lot to experience and expertise. Here are my long-term ambitions:

  • Make the technology available to everyone since everyone deserves accessing the latest technologies and information.
  • Design the new products in the way to fade the bounds of discrimination and racism so that people feel they are all part of ONE group. I believe we should get to a point where everyone would respect the Inclusion and diversity and most importantly no one would feel the loneliness.

I am interested in engineering the software in the best way possible. Designing intuitive user interfaces is also one of the fields I am very into.
